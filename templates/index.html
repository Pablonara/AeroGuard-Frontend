AeroGuard

An AI that detects weapons in airport luggage. Making the future, safer.

Our Mission

TSA check-in's are becoming too slow and inefficient and there are also some human errors involved in the TSA check-in and weapon detection. Our model helps solve those problems by being able to detect harmful weapons accurately and help reduce human error.

About the Model

We decided that our model should prioritize accuracy over efficiency, since did not want to let any potentially harmful objects through. So, we chose CNNs over Vision Transformer Models as they're known to observe even the slightest of details. This means that we would not be using YOLOs and other VIT models and instead would choose Microsoft's adaptation of Facebook’s DETR Resnet 50 model. However, we ended up choosing Facebook's original model, because of issues that we faced while trying to utilize Microsoft's adaptation.

Our final product is powered by a fined tuned version of Facebook's DETR Resnet 50 model with 150 epochs of training on 1000 X-Ray security images.
